## Landing Page Content Rules
- Eyebrow labels: uppercase, DM Sans 600, red (#E02020), letter-spacing 0.12em
- Headlines: Syne 700‚Äì800, white, tight tracking (-0.02em)
- Body: DM Sans 400, #A0A0A0, line-height 1.65
- Stats always in JetBrains Mono, white, large size
- Red is used for: CTAs, active states, eyebrows, hover borders, icons
- Never use illustrations or clipart ‚Äî use real photos, mockups, or geometric abstracts
- Section order: Nav ‚Üí Hero ‚Üí Trust Bar ‚Üí Services ‚Üí About ‚Üí Work ‚Üí Process ‚Üí Testimonials ‚Üí CTA Banner ‚Üí Footer
- Every section needs an eyebrow label above the heading
- Minimum 2 CTAs visible above the fold at all times
